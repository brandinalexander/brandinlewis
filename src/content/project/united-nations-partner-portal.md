I had the honor of working on the United Nations Partner Portal (UNPP), a pioneering web application that simplifies inter-agency collaboration for UN agencies by providing a unified platform for managing and engaging with partner organizations.

As part of the Tivix team, we focused on delivering a seamless user experience, consistent design language, and robust technical implementation to cater to the complex needs of the United Nations.
