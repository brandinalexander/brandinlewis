## Introduction

#### Background:

Nexamp, a leader in the solar energy sector, faced the challenge of streamlining their solar asset management processes. A comprehensive solution was needed to enhance efficiency and provide a cohesive platform for their expansive solar plant project portfolio.

#### ‍

#### ‍Objective:

Streamline solar asset management processes, enhance efficiency, and create a cohesive platform for Nexamp's solar plant project portfolio.

#### ‍

#### ‍My Role:

Lead Product Specialist

#### ‍Problem Statement:

*Nexamp's Challenge*: Nexamp's asset management team faced manual, inefficient data management and reporting processes for their extensive solar plant project portfolio. These processes needed optimization for scalability and third-party owner services.

#### ‍Hypothesis:

*Identifying Pain Points*: We believed that stakeholders in the owned asset value chain experienced multiple pain points related to data management, tracking obligations, automated reporting, and revenue allocation. The opportunity was to create an industry-leading asset management platform.

#### The Solution:

Collaborated with Nexamp's tech leads and subject matter experts (SME) to design a comprehensive platform.

## Key Contributions

- Orchestrated collaboration and alignment between Nexamp and the specialized team.
- Conducted user interviews to identify critical pain points and applied a jobs-to-be-done framework.
- Translated insights into user-centric designs, site architectures, and high-fidelity mockups.
- Designed an API layer to consolidate data across Nexamp's ecosystem and third-party tools.
- Developed a comprehensive budget, timeline, and roadmap.

## Outcomes

*Collaborative Success*: The engagement with Nexamp resulted in a transformative asset management platform that addressed key challenges and laid the foundation for future growth.

##### **Key Benefits for Nexamp**:

- *Efficiency Enhancement*: Nexamp's asset management processes became more efficient, reducing manual efforts and streamlining reporting.
- *Enhanced Scalability*: The platform's design allows for easy scalability, accommodating Nexamp's growing solar plant project portfolio.
- *Improved Transparency*: Real-time tracking and reporting capabilities improved transparency, enabling better decision-making.
- *Optimized Third-party Services*: The platform's features enhanced third-party owner services, further strengthening Nexamp's partnerships.

##### **Conclusion**:

The successful engagement with Nexamp not only streamlined solar asset management processes but also provided tangible benefits, enhancing efficiency, scalability, transparency, and third-party services. It exemplifies our commitment to delivering solutions that drive client success.
